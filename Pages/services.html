Services

Looking for an English language school that offers a range of services to help you improve your language skills? Look no further than English House! At English House, we offer a variety of English language services to meet your needs, including: English courses for all levels: Whether you're a beginner or an advanced speaker, we have a course that will help you improve your English language skills. Business English courses: If you need to improve your English for the workplace, our Business English courses are perfect for you. We offer courses in Business English communication, writing, and presentation skills. Exam preparation courses: If you need to take an English language exam, such as TOEFL or IELTS, we offer exam preparation courses to help you succeed. One-on-one English tutoring: For personalized attention and support, we offer one-on-one English tutoring sessions with our experienced instructors. Conversation clubs: Practice your English language skills in a relaxed and supportive environment with our conversation clubs. Online English courses: If you're unable to attend in-person classes, we offer a range of online English courses that you can take from the comfort of your own home.
